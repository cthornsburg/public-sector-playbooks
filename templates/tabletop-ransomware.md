# Tabletop Exercise: Ransomware (Outline)

## Objectives
- Validate decision-making and communications
- Confirm backup/restoration capability assumptions
- Identify gaps in logging, access, and vendor dependencies

## Scenario prompts
1) Unusual login alerts + EDR hits on a finance workstation.
2) File shares become inaccessible; ransom note appears.
3) Leadership asks: "Are we exfiltrated? Do we have to notify?"
4) Critical service impact: dispatch, utilities, permitting, payroll.

## Injects
- Vendor says: "We saw suspicious API activity."
- Citizen services are down; social media rumors begin.
- Insurance requests artifacts and timeline.
