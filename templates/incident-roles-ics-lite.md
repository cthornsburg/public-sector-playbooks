Use this to reduce chaos. One person can hold multiple roles in a small org, but **assign ownership**.

## Core roles
- **Incident Commander (IC):** overall decision-maker; sets priorities; approves major actions.
- **Operations / IT Lead:** executes containment/recovery tasks; coordinates sysadmins/MSP.
- **DFIR Lead (internal or external):** evidence preservation, triage guidance, forensic strategy.
- **Communications Lead:** internal comms + public statements; coordinates with leadership/PIO.
- **Liaison (Vendors/Partners):** ISP/MSP/cloud/insurance/legal; keeps contact log.
- **Scribe:** maintains incident log + timeline; tracks decisions + action items.

## Suggested quick RACI (starter)
| Activity | IC | Ops/IT | DFIR | Comms | Liaison | Scribe |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Declare incident / severity | A | C | C | C | C | R |
| Isolate network segments | A | R | C | I | I | I |
| Disable accounts / reset creds | A | R | C | I | I | I |
| Preserve logs / images | I | C | R | I | I | C |
| Draft initial holding statement | A | I | C | R | I | C |
| Notify leadership / stakeholders | A | C | I | R | C | C |

Legend: R=Responsible, A=Accountable, C=Consulted, I=Informed
