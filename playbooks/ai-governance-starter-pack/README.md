# AI Governance Starter Pack (City/County) — NIST AI RMF-aligned

Practical starter artifacts to help a city/county stand up AI governance quickly.

## What’s included
- **AI policy template** (NIST AI RMF-aligned): `AI_POLICY_CITY_COUNTY_NIST_AI_RMF.md`
- **1-page cheat sheet** for staff/supervisors: `AI_POLICY_CHEAT_SHEET_1P.md`
- **AI Use Case Register** (CSV template): `AI_USE_CASE_REGISTER_TEMPLATE.csv`

## Notes / disclaimer
- These are **templates** and are **not legal advice**.
- Adapt to your state laws, procurement rules, records retention, and any sector-specific requirements.
- For authoritative guidance references:
  - NIST AI RMF: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework
  - NIST AI RMF 1.0 PDF: https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/ai/NIST.AI.100-1.pdf

## Suggested 30–60 minute rollout
1) Fill the Use Case Register for any AI already in use.
2) Adopt the cheat sheet as an interim acceptable-use memo.
3) Route the full policy through your governance/legal process.
